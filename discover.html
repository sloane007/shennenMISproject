Why I chose UMD

I choose UMD because of its established business program and the reasources that the school provides to students. Additionally, I appreciate that I am able to be away from home and in a new space but can still can easily go vist family without the barrier of distance.

What I like in the surrounding areas

I absolutely love the surrounding areas of UMD, especially the nearby parks and trails as well as the easy access to travel up the north shore. Another thing that I appreciate is that we are on top of the Duluth hill making it really easy to access both the downtown area and the essential stores.

My personal experience and highlights at UMD

UMD has provided me with numerous opportunities for personal and professional growth. In the few months I have been here, I have met so many people and really been able to discover who I am personally. Additionally, I have already gotten to spend a lot of time preparing for the track meets that will begin in January.
